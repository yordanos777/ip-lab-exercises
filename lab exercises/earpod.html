True Wireless Earpods - The FX Factory

Description

Following a single-tap setup, these EarPod Pros automatically turn on and quickly connect to your phone and/or laptop; forget having to manually engage or disengage power - once paired, your earphones will automatically connect to your device when you take them out of the case (provided your Bluetooth is on). Enjoy music, answer calls, or communicate with Siri/Bixby/Google on your mobile device without having to pull out your phone.

These EarPod Pros automatically play audio as soon as you put them in your ears and pause when you take them out. A simple double-tap during music listening will skip forward. To adjust the volume, change the song, make a call, or even get directions, just say "Hey Google" to activate your favourite personal assistant.

Additionally, they deliver 4 hours of listening time on a single charge and they're made to keep up with you thanks to a wireless charging case that holds multiple additional charges for 24-28 hours. Just 5-10 minutes in the case gives you approximately an hour of listening time. Charge the case via the lightning connector cable included or take advantage of its compatibility with QI wireless charging pads/power banks/phones for true wireless charging.

Features

  • Ergonomic design and tight fit make the EarPod Pros great for working out, running, cycling

  • Wide Compatability: works with all Bluetooth enabled phones, laptops, tablets, tv's and tech devices.

  • Internally tapered tips conform to your ear shape while vents help equalize pressure for maximum comfort
